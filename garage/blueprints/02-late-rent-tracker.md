# Blueprint 02 — Late Rent Tracker

**The problem:** It's the 8th. You're not sure who paid and who didn't. You're texting
one tenant, calling another, checking your bank app. By the time you send a formal
notice you've already lost two weeks of leverage — and documentation.

**What this builds:** A monthly tracker where you mark each tenant paid or unpaid,
see at a glance who's late and by how many days, and generate a professional late
payment notice for any tenant with one click.

**Time to build:** 1–2 hours

**What to have ready:** Your rent due date, your grace period, your late fee policy,
and a list of your tenants and units.

I want to build a late rent tracker for my property management business.

Here's the problem: every month I'm manually tracking who paid and who didn't
across texts, my bank, and my memory. By the time I send a formal notice I've
already lost days — and I have no written record that I tried to reach them.

Here's exactly what I want it to do:

1. Show me all my tenants for the current month with a simple paid / unpaid status
   I can update in a few clicks
2. For anyone unpaid, show me how many days past due they are
3. For anyone who needs a formal notice, generate a professional late payment letter
   that already has their name, unit, amount owed, and days past due filled in —
   ready for me to copy and send

I want a record of when I sent notices. If this ever ends up in front of a judge,
I want to be able to show I followed the right process.

Keep it simple. I'm not trying to automate this — I want to stay in control of when
notices go out. I just want the paperwork to take two minutes instead of twenty.

Please follow the normal build process: tell me what you're going to build
before you write anything. I want to approve the plan first.

## After the Build

Run this on the 5th of every month:
- Mark who's paid and who hasn't
- Generate notices for anyone past your grace period
- Send the notices — you have a record of the date automatically

**Result:** Rent collection becomes a 10-minute task. You have documentation
for every late notice ever sent.
